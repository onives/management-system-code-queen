The Children is a UK registered charity that focuses on empowering young women through education and training that increases their employability. Last year we launched Code Queen in Kampala which is an all-female bootcamp designed to train young women in coding and soft skills. Code Queen has successfully run 3 cohorts and graduated 70 students since the launch in 2019.
